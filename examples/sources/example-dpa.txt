ACME Corp — Data Processing Addendum (example / fictional), Section 6: Sub-processors & Residency.

6.1 The Processor shall not engage any sub-processor to process Customer Personal Data without the
Controller's prior written authorization.

6.2 The Processor shall process and store Customer Personal Data only within the European Economic
Area. Transfer of Customer Personal Data outside the EEA is prohibited absent an approved transfer
mechanism.

6.3 Upon termination, the Processor shall delete or return all Customer Personal Data within 30 days
and certify such deletion in writing.
